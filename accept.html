"Well, you can choose to live a boring and normal life, but I will find ways and means to entertain myself," the voice continues.

You feel several wet, squelching sensations on your body as the voice finishes. Once again, you are left to contemplate.

Real, grounded, and tough. A life where each choice matters, where suffering and joy walk hand-in-hand. But is this balance what you want? Do you have the strength to bear the weight of such a life?

A thrill, not your own pulses through you as you consider the Deadly path. Survival will be a battle every day, and danger will lurk around every corner. But you could achieve greatness... or perish in an instant.
